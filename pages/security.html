Trust & Safety

EireID is built on the globally recognized Zero Trust architecture, ensuring your identity remains yours alone.

Defense in Depth

Biometric Locking

Your EireID is hardware-locked to your device's Secure Enclave. Access requires your unique biometric signature, verified locally without ever leaving your device.

Data Sovereignty

You own your data. EireID uses end-to-end encryption for all transfers, meaning not even the Irish Government can access your private documents without your explicit consent.

PPSN Shield

Unlike physical cards, EireID uses dynamic tokens to verify your PPSN. This prevents identity theft and ensures that your sensitive numbers are never exposed to third parties.
