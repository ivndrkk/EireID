How it works

EireID changes how you manage your identity. We don't store your documents; instead, we hold secure, verifiable credentials and encrypted access keys that only you can use.

Create your identity

Sign up and verify your identity in minutes with a secure, one-time onboarding process.

Connect your documents

Securely link your passport, PSC, and other official documents directly from trusted sources.

Credentials, not files

We store encrypted keys and verifiable references, not your raw files. Your privacy remains yours.

Share instantly

Grant instant, secure access to banks, landlords, or employers with a single tap in the app.

Total control

You own your data. Manage permissions and revoke access at any time with full transparency.
