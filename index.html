How it works

EireID changes how you manage your identity. We don't store your documents; instead, we hold secure, verifiable credentials and encrypted access keys that only you can use.

Create your EireID account

Download the app and sign up in minutes. No paperwork — just a secure account to hold your digital identity.

Enter your PPS or document number

Type in your PPS number, passport, or any supported ID number — or connect your bank account for instant verification.

How We Protect Your Data

Your privacy is built by design. We use advanced encryption and strict data minimization to ensure your information is secure and only used for its intended purpose.

Encryption (Transit & Rest)

All data is encrypted in transit using TLS 1.3 and at rest with AES-256-GCM. We use hardware-bound keys only accessible by your device.

Data Minimization

We collect only the absolute minimum data required to verify your identity. We never profile or track your interactions.

EU Data Sovereignty

All identity records are stored in high-security Irish data centers, adhering strictly to GDPR and national regulations.

On-Device First

Primary identity processing happens on your device's Secure Enclave, ensuring raw documents never leave your hands.

Identity & Authentication

Accessing your identity is seamless but highly protected. We utilize multi-layered authentication tied to your physical hardware.

Secure Authentication Layers

  • Biometric Verification: Local-only authentication via FaceID or TouchID sensors.
  • Hardware Multi-Factor: Cryptographic verification tied to secure physical tokens.
  • Root Key Binding: Your digital soul is cryptographically tied to the device's unique silicon fingerprint.

Privacy Note: Your biometric identifiers (face map or fingerprints) never leave your device's hardware chip. EireID servers never touch your biological data.
