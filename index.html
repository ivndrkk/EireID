How it works

EireID changes how you manage your identity. We don't store your documents; instead, we hold secure, verifiable credentials and encrypted access keys that only you can use.

Create your EireID account

Download the app and sign up in minutes. No paperwork — just a secure account to hold your digital identity.

Enter your PPS or document number

Type in your PPS number, passport, or any supported ID number — or connect your bank account for instant verification.

Biometric face check

A quick one-time face scan confirms it's really you — no passwords, no ID photos to upload, just a glance.

Your credentials are fetched securely

EireID connects to official government servers and retrieves only your verified credentials — never your raw documents or files.

All your IDs, ready to share

Your documents appear instantly in your digital wallet. Share verified credentials with a tap — and revoke access anytime.
